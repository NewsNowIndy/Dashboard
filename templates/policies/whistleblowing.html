Purpose & Scope

NewsNowIndy Tips exists to receive and investigate information in the public interest, including public corruption, misuse of funds, conflicts of interest, failures in public safety systems, systemic misconduct, and similar matters affecting residents of Central Indiana.

Who May Submit

Anyone with credible information (employees, contractors, public officials, community members) may submit tips. You may remain anonymous.

Submission Guidelines

What to submit:

  • Factual descriptions, dates, names of entities, locations, and relevant documents.
  • Where possible, corroborating evidence and an explanation of how you know.

What not to submit:

  • Classified information or material you are legally prohibited from sharing.
  • Sensitive personal data about private individuals unless essential to demonstrate a significant public-interest issue.
  • Malware or password-protected archives without including the password.

How We Handle Submissions

  • Every tip is triaged for credibility and public-interest value.
  • We seek corroboration via documents, data, and on-the-record sources before publication.
  • We minimize sensitive personal data and consider potential harm before publishing.
  • If you provide contact details, we may follow up with questions.

Confidentiality & Security

GlobaLeaks is designed to minimize logs and protect sources. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to safeguard submissions. Absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

Retention

We keep submissions only as long as needed for reporting, legal, security, or archival purposes, after which we delete or further anonymize them.

Editorial Independence & Legal

Decisions about coverage are made by NewsNowIndy editors in accordance with journalistic standards.

Legal: We comply with applicable U.S. laws and may challenge legal demands for source information where feasible. Nothing here constitutes legal advice.

Abuse

We may restrict or remove submissions that are fraudulent, abusive, or outside the public interest.
